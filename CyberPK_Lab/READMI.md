⚡ CYBER_PK // Adaptive Communication Buffer
An experimental interface built to protect cognitive energy and eliminate the friction of social masking. It helps neurodivergent individuals process incoming messages and draft replies without the executive burnout.

The Core Stack:
Intent Decryption (LLM): Powered by gemini-2.5-flash to slice through walls of text and instantly generate three pragmatic, ready-to-send options: Direct, Cordial, or a Graceful Exit.

High-Contrast UI: A dark AMOLED, cyberpunk-inspired Flet interface calibrated specifically to reduce visual fatigue and ease reading distortions.

Zero-Friction Piping: Direct asynchronous integration with your system clipboard—paste, process, copy, and leave. No extra steps.
