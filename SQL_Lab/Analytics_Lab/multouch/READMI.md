# Multi-Touch Attribution Engine

A multi-touch attribution calculator built with Python to analyze customer conversion journeys. This tool helps businesses understand which marketing channels (Google Ads, Facebook Ads, TikTok, etc.) actually drive revenue by distributing purchase credit using data-backed mathematical models.

## Attribution Models

The engine calculates revenue allocation across five distinct logic layers:

* **First Click:** Allocates 100% of the revenue credit to the very first marketing channel the user touched.
* **Last Click:** Allocates 100% of the revenue credit to the final marketing channel before conversion.
* **Linear:** Splits the conversion revenue equally among all touchpoints in the customer journey.
* **Time Decay:** Gives more credit to touchpoints closer to the conversion time, using a 7-day half-life decay formula.
* **Data-Driven (Markov Chains):** Uses state transition probabilities to model paths to conversion or dropoff. It computes the unique *removal effect* of each channel to distribute revenue dynamically based on your actual data.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Architecture

* **UI Framework:** **Streamlit** featuring a custom dark-themed interface and responsive controls.
* **Database Engine:** **SQLAlchemy 2.0 (Async)** over **aiosqlite**, utilizing `NullPool` to prevent event loop and thread conflicts during live page refreshes.
* **Data Validation:** **Pydantic V2** to enforce strict type checking and schema validation before data enters the calculation matrix.
* **Data Visualization:** **Plotly Express** for side-by-side dark-mode bar and donut charts comparing ad spend against model revenues.
* **Data Processing:** **Pandas** and **NumPy** for matrix manipulation, array operations, and mock data distribution.
* **Logging:** **Loguru** for clean, structured console outputs.

## Database Schema

The SQLite database consists of two tables managed via an async SQLAlchemy Declarative Base:

### `customer_journeys`
* `id` *(Integer, PK)*: Autoincrementing record identifier.
* `user_id` *(String)*: Unique identifier for the user.
* `touchpoints` *(Text)*: Semi-colon separated list of marketing channels (e.g., `google;facebook`).
* `timestamps` *(Text)*: Semi-colon separated list of Unix timestamps corresponding to the touchpoints.
* `revenue` *(Float)*: Total monetary value generated by the conversion.
* `converted` *(Integer)*: Binary flag where `1` means converted and `0` means dropped off.

### `channel_costs`
* `id` *(Integer, PK)*: Autoincrementing record identifier.
* `channel` *(String)*: Unique marketing channel name.
* `cost` *(Float)*: Total marketing spend allocated to that channel.
